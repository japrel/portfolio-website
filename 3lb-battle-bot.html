3 lb Battle Bot Specifications

The robot was designed and built for the 3 lb combat robot category.

Chassis and Structure

  • Base Plate: 3 mm thick polycarbonate sheet
  • Side Walls: 2 mm thick L-shaped aluminum sections
  • The side walls were attached to the base plate using rivets, providing a lightweight yet rigid structure.

Weapon System

  • Weapon Material: Aluminum
  • Weapon Teeth: Cast iron
  • The weapon was mounted on a 5 mm steel rod shaft for rotation.
  • Weapon Motor: 1250 KV Turnigy brushless motor
  • ESC: RedBrick 60 A Electronic Speed Controller
  • Battery: 4S 800 mAh Li-Po battery

Drive System

  • Drive Motors: 300 RPM Johnson geared motors
  • Motor Controllers: Modified 20D ESCs
  • Wheels: Polyurethane wheels with a diameter of 7.5 cm

Control System

  • Transmitter: FlySky FS-i6
  • Receiver: 2.4 GHz 6-channel receiver

Working Principle

The robot uses two 300 RPM geared motors for differential drive movement. The spinning weapon is powered by a 1250 KV brushless motor controlled through a 60 A ESC. The cast iron teeth attached to the aluminum weapon increase impact force during combat. Control signals are transmitted from the FlySky FS-i6 transmitter to the receiver, which operates both the drive and weapon systems.
